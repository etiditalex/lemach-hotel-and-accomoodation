Getting Here

By Road: Located just off B69 highway, easily accessible from Mombasa and Malindi.

By Air: Nearest airport is Mombasa International Airport (MBA), approximately 1.5 hours drive.

By Public Transport: Regular matatu services available from major towns in the region.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we provide airport transfer services from Mombasa International Airport. Please contact us in advance to arrange this service.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer free parking for all our guests. The parking area is secure and monitored 24/7.

Do you accept pets?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets in our hotel. We can recommend nearby pet-friendly accommodations if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), and bank transfers.

Do you have WiFi?

Yes, we provide complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the hotel for all our guests.
